Open Source Hardware & Tools for Machines

We are embarking on a journey to provide open source hardware to make common tools and machines easier to use and talk to. We are providing CAD plans, PCBs, schematics, and products that enhance the abilities of machines and provide people with data and feedback. We hope to increase use of tools, provide safer environments, and increase the longevity of machines common to woodworking, metalworking, CNC, and more!
